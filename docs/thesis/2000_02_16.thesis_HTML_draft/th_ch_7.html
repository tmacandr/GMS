The second category, Arc Digitized Raster Graphics (ADRG), is essentially a digitization of "paper maps". NIMA has a very large library of paper maps of various regions of the world and to varying degrees of accuracy. Several government-sponsored projects were implemented to digitally scan these paper maps, thereby converting the "paper" library to a digital library.

These paper maps were In other words, is used to generate "paper-maps" on the computer screen. The data for these maps were generated by digitizing the paper maps used by the U.S. Department of Defense. There are several subclasses of ADRG maps. Some are:

  • JNC - Jet Navigational Chart
  • ONC - Operational Navigational Chart
  • TPC - Tactical Pilotage Chart
  • JOG - Joint Operations Graphic
  • TLM - Tactical Line Map
  • City Graphic

Each map-type has varying degrees of accuracy. Some map-types are used by aviators and other are used by surveyors, etc.
